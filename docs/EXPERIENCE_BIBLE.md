# Experience Bible

## North Star
Build a physical place inside a browser.

## Core Principles
1. The room is real.
2. The room never performs.
3. The camera is a human body.
4. The room is larger than the browser.
5. The room obeys physics.
6. Only the image questions reality.
7. Reality occasionally leaks.
8. Reward attention, not clicks.
9. Never explain the trick.
10. Confidence over cleverness.

## Camera Verbs
- Sit
- Lean
- Stand
- Turn

## Ambient Life
The room has momentum.
Nothing responds to hover.

## Leaks
The impossible should happen where the viewer least expects it.
